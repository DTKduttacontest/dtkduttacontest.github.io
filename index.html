DTK Dutta Contest

An annual contest reconnecting Bhagalpur's youth with their heritage through research, curiosity, and inquiry

Latest: Our research contest has been featured by Citizen Times (see here) and Jagran (here).
Update: Winners of the 2025 contest have been announced!

The Contest

This contest introduces Bhagalpur’s high school students to scientific and academic research by encouraging them to explore the city’s socio-economic and cultural landscape. Participants can win cash prizes of up to INR 1 lakh, along with mementos and certificates.

Learn more

The Mentors

Mentors guide students through the process of research and discovery. They help students transform questions about their heritage into thoughtful research.
